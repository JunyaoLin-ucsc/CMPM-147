Description

A pair of procedural generators: one for an overworld with smoothly blended biomes, and one for a dungeon composed of rectangular rooms and corridors.

Technical

Generation is separated from rendering: each seed produces a grid of single-character codes. Autotiling logic computes transition tiles based on neighbors. The editable text area updates the scene immediately. Animated variation and perlin-noise–based effects use p5.js’s noise(), random(), and millis().

Reflection

Implementing bitmask autotiling deepened my understanding of spatial data structures and neighbor-based algorithms. Integrating live text editing revealed challenges in syncing state. Future improvements include mouse-driven interactions and richer tile decorations.
